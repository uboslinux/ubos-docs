Get in touch!

We'd love to have your feedback, suggestions, and will be happy to help with anything you need. Just get in touch!

  • Follow @uboslinux on Twitter and tweet to/about us.
  • Chat with us on IRC at #ubos on freenode.
  • Fork a UBOS repository on github, file a bug or feature request, or submit a pull request.
    Here are some specific ideas you could do if you'd like to help us.
  • Visit us during UBOS office hours.

We will also be happy to help you getting involved in the project. Get in touch and say Hi to get started.
