Get in touch!

We'd love to have your feedback, suggestions, and will be happy to help with anything you need. Just get in touch!

  • Follow UBOS on Twitter. Follow @uboslinux
  • Chat with us on IRC at #ubos on freenode.
  • Fork a UBOS repository on github, file a bug or feature request, or submit a pull request.
  • Subscribe to the development mailing list.
  • Visit us during Indie Box/UBOS office hours.

We will also be happy to help you getting involved in the project. Subscribe to the mailing list and say Hi to get started.
